About me

I was born in 1987 in Siófok, Hungary.

I studied graphic art and painting for seven years at various institutions, but the experience was so disheartening that I stopped painting for ten years.

In 2023, I moved to the Netherlands and began painting again.

Unlike my earlier work, I now focus exclusively on classical-style paintings on canvas. My favorite subjects are old brick buildings or vibrant landscapes.

I can create a painting from a photo of a building if you like, but I don't do portraits.
