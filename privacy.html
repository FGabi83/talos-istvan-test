Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how your personal data is collected, used, and protected when you visit the website of István Tálos. We respect your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information.

1. Personal Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data automatically through this website. There are no cookies, tracking systems, or analytics tools in place.

The only personal data we may collect is what you voluntarily provide when contacting the artist via the email address provided on the site.

2. How We Use Your Data

If you contact the artist by email, the personal data you provide (such as your name and email address) will be used only for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries about the available paintings or to discuss custom art commissions.
  • To process and arrange the purchase of artwork.

3. Data Sharing

Your personal data will not be shared with any third parties, except if required by law or to comply with legal obligations.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect any personal data you provide through email correspondence. However, please be aware that no method of data transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction, or deletion of the personal data that you have shared with us. To exercise any of these rights, please contact the artist via the email provided on the website.

6. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please feel free to contact the artist at: info@talos-istvan.com
